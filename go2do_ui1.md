UI Design Specification: Minimalist Black Todo System (Updated)
===============================================================

Design Philosophy
-----------------

**Principles:**

*   Black background (#0a0a0a) - reduces eye strain, looks professional
    
*   White text (#ffffff) for maximum contrast
    
*   Subtle grays for secondary elements
    
*   **Strategic color usage:** Red, yellow, green only where meaning is clear
    
*   **Smooth animations:** Fades, slides, scale - but fast (100-200ms)
    
*   Geist font family throughout
    
*   No bounce/spring/complex animations
